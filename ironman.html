Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional superhero appearing in Marvel Comics. Created by writer Stan Lee, he first appeared in "Tales of Suspense" #39 in 1963. Tony Stark is a genius billionaire, inventor, and philanthropist who builds a high-tech armored suit to fight against evil. As a founding member of the Avengers, Iron Man plays a crucial role in protecting the world from powerful threats.
