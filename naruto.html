Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is the main character of the Japanese manga and anime series "Naruto," created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a young ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the strongest ninja and leader of his village. Despite facing hardships, he never gives up and becomes a hero known for his courage, determination, and strong belief in friendship. Naruto masters powerful techniques like the Rasengan and Sage Mode, playing a crucial role in saving the world.

  • Rasengan
